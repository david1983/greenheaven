Green Heaven

Family restaurant


Sitting just behind the farm we have our restaurant venue wich, back in the 1800, was a stable for horses.
Inspired by great artisanal foods of the world. We craft all of our stocks, sauces, breads, pastries and ice creams from scratch on a daily basis, You will be delighted by amazing dishes prepared by our italian chef Davide.
