Green Heaven

Green Heaven is one of London's largest and longest established city farms. At Green Heaven we believe that "you are what you eat", so we like to grow in our fields a large range of fresh, delicious and healthy products and use them in our restaurant.
The farm include also a shop where you will find our range of fresh picked vegetables and fruits, and our award-winning cheeses. We also provide guided tours of the farm, in where you will learn different activities.
